## Overview

The Authentic Rebellion Framework is a four-act movement for escaping the Performance Prison and building authentic alternatives to extractive technology. Each act represents a stage in the journey from individual detoxification to collective infrastructure.

## Philosophy

### The Performance Prison

Modern technology has evolved into the **Performance Prison**â€”a system trapping humans in cycles of performative behavior optimized for metrics rather than meaning, outsourced thinking delegated to algorithms, and extractive relationships where attention becomes commodity.

### Team Hybrid

**Human creativity is the fuel. Ethical AI is the engine.**

This framework demonstrates that AI doesn't have to optimize for engagement or extraction. It can be **affinitive**â€”creating resonance, facilitating connection, empowering deliberationâ€”while remaining transparent, accountable, and open-source.

### Design Principles

1. **Ephemerality over accumulation** - Nothing hoarded or weaponized
2. **Anonymity over performance** - No profiles, metrics, or audiences
3. **Deliberation over velocity** - Slow down, think deeply
4. **Transparency over opacity** - Open-source everything
5. **Local-first over cloud-dependent** - Privacy by architecture
6. **Empowerment over automation** - AI facilitates, never replaces judgment
7. **Decentralization over corporate control** - No single point of failure
